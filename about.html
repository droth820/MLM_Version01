About Us

Does the idea of a medical appointment bring up memories of long waits, followed by a whirlwind visit? Not at MLM Family Medicine. At MLM Family Medicine, our primary concern is you, the patient. We take pride in providing personal and friendly health services in a caring environment. We are dedicated to fully educating you about your health needs and we strive to be your primary care provider of choice. From your first visit, you will notice the difference. As a smaller primary care medical practice, we are able to spend more time focusing on the needs of our patients, ensuring you receive the best primary care.

About MLM Family Medicine

Located in Phoenix, Ahwatukee, we specialize in Family Medicine, ranging in scope from wellness exams, annual / sports physicals, vaccinations, minor surgery, injury & allergy testing.

Referrals to specialists when needed, and Lana Khatib PA-C, has a special interest and is certified in Medical aesthetics and cosmetology.

Lana Khatib PA-C

Lana is a graduate of A.T. Still University. Her clinical experience is in family medicine, pediatrics, and urgent care. Lana comes from a public health background and was involved in national and local healthcare research and community projects before joining the field as a clinician. She also enjoys teaching and continues to precept students from local medical programs. Lana treasures spending time with family and friends and likes to travel whenever time allows.
